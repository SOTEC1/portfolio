A Few Accomplishments

SCVNGR

SCVNGR is a full stack job board app that allows users to keep track and organize their list of potential employeers. Using drag and drop functionality users can move job cards to different sections on the screen to show what stage of the process they are in. Click the picture to go to the site!

Dream Catcher

Dream Catcher is a full stack CRUD application where users are allowed to create an account, then using native speech can record their dreams and track their sleep. All data is stored in a MySQL database. Click the picture to go to the site!
